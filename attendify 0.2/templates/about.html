About Attendify

Face recognition based attendance management system.

What is Attendify?

Attendify is a modern, AI-powered attendance management system that uses facial recognition technology to automatically mark student attendance. It's designed for schools, colleges, and training centers.

Key Features

  • Face Recognition Attendance - Auto-mark attendance using camera
  • Student Management - Add, edit, delete students
  • Reports & Analytics - Track attendance trends
  • Email Verification - OTP for student registration
  • Export Reports - Download attendance data
  • Low Attendance Alerts - Get notified
  • Mobile Responsive - Works on phones
  • Backup & Restore - Database management

Technology Stack

  • Backend: Python Flask
  • Database: SQLite
  • Face Recognition: dlib + face_recognition
  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript
  • Camera: OpenCV
